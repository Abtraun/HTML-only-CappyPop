CappyPop Help and Support

Thank you for choosing CappyPop! We're here to make your experience as enjoyable as possible.

General FAQs

What are your store hours and location?

Our store is at HAU and open from 10 AM to 10 PM, Monday through Sunday

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events

Please feel free to contact us at @CappyPop.HAU or at our Live Chat Support for more information.

Product FAQs

What ingredients are used in your boba tea?

We use high-quality ingredients, including fresh tea leaves, premium tapioca pearls, and natural flavors

For specific ingredient details, please refer to the product descriptions on our website. We also offer dairy and non-dairy milk options to cater to different dietary preferences.

Do you offer any vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegan and gluten-free options

Our fruit teas and many of our milk alternatives are vegan-friendly, and our tapioca pearls are gluten-free. Please check the product descriptions for specific details or contact us for more information.

Ordering and Payment FAQs

How do I place an order online?

You can place an order through our website by selecting your desired items and proceeding to checkout

Remember to follow the prompts to complete your order. You can also find us on Grap Food, Be Food and Baemin

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Momo, VN Pay and Zalo Pay

You can choose option Pay with Cash in Your Profile/Payment methods.

Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?

You can change or cancel your Order before checking out

Once the order has been processed, we may not be able to make changes.

Shipping and Delivery FAQs

How much does the shipping cost and How long does delivery take?

Shipping costs and delivery time are based on your location, usually from 10k to 50k . We guarante the delivery time to be from 10 mins to 20 mins

You can apply coupon code to get discount and free shipping from us. Please contact us at @BubblePop.HAU for more information.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order directly on your Profile tracking page

Go to your Profile/Your Order/Delivery and click on the Track my Order button. The link to tracking page is here.

Technical issues FAQs

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password

We will send a comfirmation code to your email or phone number. You will enter the code and reset your password later on.

How do I update my account information?

First you need to log in to your account and go to Your Profile page

In there you can start to update your personal information, shipping address, and payment method details.

What should I do if I'm experiencing technical issues with the website?

Our site works best with Chrome and Safari

If you are experiencing technical issues, please try clearing your browser cache and cookies. If the problem persists, contact us at @CappyPop.HAU or at our Live Chat Support with a description of the issue.
